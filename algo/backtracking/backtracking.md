### What is Backtracking ?
Backtracking is an improvement of the brute force approach.
It iteratively searches for a solution to a problem among all available options.

Backtracking is a form of recursion, usually we are faced with number of options and we must choose one.
After we make our choice we will get a new set of options, the set of options depends on what choice we made.

If we backtracked all the way to our initial state and have explored all alternatives from there , we can conclude
the particular problem is unsolvable.
